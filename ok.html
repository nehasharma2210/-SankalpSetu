We are thrilled to invite PIZZA HUT to collaborate with us for OJASS'25, our flagship annual national-level techno-management fest, scheduled from 14th -16th February, 2025.

OJASS has evolved into one of India's most prestigious college fests, attracting 15,000+ footfall, 85,000+ followers, 25,00,000+ online impressions, and participation from institutions across the country in 50+ events. This fest also features an exciting Moto Expo, Auto Expo, Drone Show, Laser Show, a lively Book fair, Influencer meetup with 100+ influencers across different domains, and showcases local culture through vibrant cultural stalls and immersive exhibits. 

Over the years, OJASS has had the privilege of hosting esteemed guests such as Late Mr. Om Puri (Actor), Ash King (Singer), Gulshan Grover (Actor), Ravinder Singh (Writer), Zakir Khan (Comedian), Mandira Bedi (Actress & Presenter), and Varun Agrawal (Entrepreneur) among many others.
